Title
Michael Rapciak, Scott Mitchell, Daniel Mendioza


Elevator pitch 
Need For Speed In Space will be an action racing game set in the vast danger of space.
You will have to upgrade your vehicle to last longer in space races as you try and become the
greatest driver to do it in this new frontier of racing.
...
World Background
The world will be based on the outer space. It will include tracks on asteroid belts and space ships.
The society is more advanced than ours but the technology is not extremely developed - Low Sci-Fi.
The setting of the story will be a very competitive one - fighting for the top prize of being the
best driver in the world and getting the biggest prizes.
...
Pillars
Satisfying sence of speed, graphics that sell the rocket motion, tight controls to match
the fantay of greater speeds than our world
...
30 second gameplay description
The game experience resembles the known franchise Need for Speed but in this installment
the adrenaline comes with rocket propelled shutters going at greater velocity which makes
the gameplay interesting and dangerously addictive.
...
Features points
Racing in Space, Combat whilst racing, Online Multiplayer,
Coop mode, Career Mode, Exhibition Mode, Create your own Car.
...
Market analysis
Similar gameplay style to star wars squadrons but with a racing twist, Our game would be suited 
towards sci-fi lovers whom also like a racing game, The sales we expect are around 250 Thousand 
copies in the first quater.
